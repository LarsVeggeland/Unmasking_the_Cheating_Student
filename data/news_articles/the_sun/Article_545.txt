Author: Alice Fuller
POLICE are urgently searching for a mum who is missing with her five-year-old son.
Kelly, 28, and little Harvey were last seen leaving New Cross Hospital in Wolverhampton, West Midlands, at around 7.50am today. 
Cops are out searching for the pair and have appealed to the public to help find them. 
Kelly is described as 5ft 3in with brown hair. 
She was wearing leopard print trousers and a parker coat when she disappeared.
Harvey has blonde hair and was last seen wearing a black coat.
A spokesperson for Wolverhampton Police said: "We are urgently appealing for a mum who is missing with her son to get in touch. 
"We have officers out searching for the pair and would urge anyone with information on their whereabouts to get in touch as soon as possible."
Anyone with information should call 999 quoting quoting log 709 of January 21.
